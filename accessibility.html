Accessibility Statement

We have implemented access keys throughout our website according to the following scheme:
1: Index
2: Founders
3: News
4: Recommendations
5: Puzzles
9: Contact
m: Match the Pairs
k: Accessibility

These follow the standard access keys given at Standard Access Keys where possible.

We have also tried to follow the RNIB guidelines for making our page accessible to the blind, including using an empty alt tag on our decorative images in order to avoid giving extra noise to screen readers.

Finally, our distinctive colour scheme is chosen to help avoid making our page inaccessible to those who are red-green colourblind.

We endeavour to make our website perceivable, operable, understandable and robust, in accordance with the WCAG guidelines. If we have failed to do this in any way do not hesitate to contact us.
